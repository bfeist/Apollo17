This is a real-time interactive exploration of the Apollo 17 Mission.
You can read more about this project here. Follow @BenFeist for updates.

Instructions:

  • Chrome / Firefox / Safari preferred. Works on iPad with patience.
  • Click transcript items and playback will jump to that moment.
  • For big jumps, use the mission navigator at the top of the page (or table of contents tab)
  • Jump to different points using the video scrubber and playback will jump to that moment.

Project History:

  • 10/27/2015: v1.0b2 Complete rework of interface. Introduced navigator element. Cross-device compatibility.
  • 05/08/2015: v0.6 Social sharing of a given moment added. Also, several transcript corrections thanks to Robin Wheeler's assistance.
  • 04/14/2015: v0.5 Commentary and imagery updates in real-time. Commentary for first day online. Imagery ongoing.
  • 04/08/2015: v0.4 Ongoing optimizations. Began adding commentary and imagery.
  • 03/28/2015: v0.3 Greatly improved table of contents. Corrected some transcription and video errors.
  • 03/26/2015: v0.2 Improvements to TOC. JavaScript stability improvements.
  • 03/25/2015: v0.1 Initial launch made public.

Note: This is an early alpha release of this project. I have been working on cleaning the underlying mission data for many years. My goal is to create a full-featured site that will allow the public to explore and experience the Apollo 17 mission in full detail. In the mean time, please don't hesitate to contact me with any questions or comments at bf@benfeist.com

Planned Features:

  • Creation of visual designs for final interface, site introduction and ancillary mission information.
