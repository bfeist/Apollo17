This is a real-time interactive exploration of the entire Apollo 17 Mission.
You can read more about this project here. Follow @BenFeist for updates.

Instructions:

  • Desktop only. Use Google Chrome (this hasn't been tested on any other browser).
  • Click any line in the transcript and the video will jump to that instant.
  • For big jumps, click an item in the table of contents (bottom left).
  • Alternatively, jump to different points on the YouTube video and the transcript will scroll to match.

Note: This is an early alpha release of this project. I have been working on cleaning the underlying mission data for many years. My goal is to create a full-featured site that will allow the public to explore and experience the Apollo 17 mission in full detail. In the mean time, please don't hesitate to contact me with any questions or comments at bf@benfeist.com

Planned Features:

  • Interactive visual mission timeline navigation interface (bottom of screen).
  • Creation of visual designs for final interface, site introduction and ancillary mission information.
  • Bookmarking and discussion notes for any timeline moment.
